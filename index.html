Video Sim - Fast Imaging - Adam Wise 2019

What is this? Above are windows simulating a sub-area of each camera, acquiring 16-bit image data as quickly as possible. Each window shows what a small Gaussian spot focused on the camera would look like, with the size of the spot scaled to match the cameras pixel size. "Signal peak" is the number of photons per pixel at the brightest part of the spot. Frame rates are relative.
